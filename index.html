About Me

Where AI meets biology — decoding life's operating system

I'm passionate about applying cutting-edge deep learning to solve fundamental problems in genomics and epigenomics. My work sits at the intersection of computational biology, machine learning, and systems biology — building models that learn from DNA sequence, chromatin state, and transcription to understand how cells work.
